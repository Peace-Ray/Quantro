Join or create an Internet Lobby. Tap a lobby to join it; long-press to examine lobby details. Tap the 'X' to remove a lobby from the list.

Internet Multiplayer requires an active Internet connection.

Quantro's multiplayer features are currently in BETA. Availability is not guaranteed; you may experience bugs or connection failures.
