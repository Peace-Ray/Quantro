Play Quantro's multiplayer game modes and chat with other players between games. Use Android Beam™ to share this lobby with other players.

You'll stay in the lobby even if you leave this app with the 'Home' button. Use the 'Back' button to disconnect from the lobby.

Quantro's multiplayer features are currently in BETA. Availability is not guaranteed; you may experience bugs or connection failures.


Vote

Vote for game modes. Games will launch automatically when enough players have cast their votes. Votes are indicated by colored checkmarks; colored marks on the right edge of the screen indicate votes which have been scrolled out of view.


Chat

Chat with other players in this lobby.


Invite Players

Send invitations to your friends to join this lobby. Available only for Internet Lobbies.


Lobby Info

Review specific information about this lobby, including local IP address (if applicable) and time spent open.


Settings

Configure settings, including tooltips, graphical quality, animations, volume, controls, etc.
